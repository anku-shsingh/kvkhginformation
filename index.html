Examination Notice

Subject: Submission of PT-1 Question Papers

Subject teachers are kindly requested to submit their PT-1 Question Papers to the Examination Section According to notice No - 49 Date-02.07.2025 by 15 July 2025 without fail.

Ensure the paper is properly formatted and includes the marking scheme. For any queries, contact the Examination I/C. Or Member of Examination

Date: 15 July 2025


Subject: Postponement of PT-1 Test Dates

All are hereby informed that due to administrative reasons, the scheduled dates for PT-1 Test have been postponed.

The test will now begin from 30 July 2025 instead of 28 July 2025. Therefore, PT-1 examinations should be conducted between 30 July to 6 August 2025 in each cluster.

Furthermore, the Parent-Teacher Meeting (PTM) for PT-1 must be completed by 12 August 2025.

All concerned are requested to take note and make necessary arrangements accordingly.

Date: 15 July 2025

Subject: Urgent Attention on Samagam, UDISE+ and UBI Work

This is to inform all class teachers that any work related to Samagam, UDISE+, or UBI is being strictly monitored either by HQs or directly by the Ministry of Education (MoE).

All class teachers are requested to give top priority to these tasks. The submitted data must be accurate and error-free as each class teacher will be held accountable for the data of their respective class.

Your cooperation is highly appreciated in maintaining the integrity and timely completion of these activities.
